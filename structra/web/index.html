What Structra does

We convert domain documents into automation-ready datasets.

Most teams already have the data they need, but it is trapped inside reports, case files, study notes, SOPs, policy documents, and websites. Structra pulls that content into one workflow and makes it usable for machine learning and fine-tuning.

Extract

Bring in messy domain content.

Upload PDFs and Word documents or fetch public websites to capture the raw knowledge your team already works with.

Transform

Clean, chunk, and score for quality.

We remove weak chunks, control overlap, and reduce duplicates so the dataset is more useful before generation even starts.

Generate

Create datasets for ML or fine-tuning.

Route the cleaned source into structured ML records or Grok-powered ChatML and Alpaca fine-tuning datasets.
